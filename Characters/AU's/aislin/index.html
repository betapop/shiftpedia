    Aislin

    • Name Aislin

      Farm Name Bumblebee Farm

      Age 21

      Birthday Fall 19

      Interests Polishing Geodes, Sketching, Sewing


      Best Gifts Salmon Dinner, Seafoam Pudding, Aquamarine

    Personality

    High energy and outgoing. They are naturally charismatic, but a bit too socially awkward to use it. When they're working, they can be extremely focused and calculating. Loves to talk on and on to loved ones.

    Biography

    Aislin and Estrella grew up together in Zuzu City. Their parents were usually away, so both of them grew with many life skills under their belts. After much thought, they decided to move out of the city and move into the Valley. While they were there, they met Ember, a newly moved in farmer who inherented her grandfathers farm. As they had nowhere to go, Ember decided to let them live there as long as they paid rent and helped with the farm. Aislin was extremely happy with this predicament. They had always been the more messy sibling, so doing more messy work worked out perfectly for them.
