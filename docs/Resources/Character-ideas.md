Some of my favorite things to do with my ocs! Of course a great thing you can do is write that story. I know, its hard, but it'll be worth it :) In the meantime though, try one of these!

## No Drawing/Writing
- Make a shrine ([Example](https://www.pinterest.com/search/pins/?q=oc%20shrine&rs=typed))
- Make a personality [quiz](https://uquiz.com/quiz-maker)
- Make an [UTAU](https://utau.fandom.com/wiki/UTAU_wiki)
- Make merch ([manufacturers](http://127.0.0.1:8000/shiftpedia/Resources/Shopping/#manufacturers)/[homemade](#))
- Make a playlist for them
- Make an OC Binder
- Make [memes](https://www.pinterest.com/search/pins/?q=meme%20template&rs=typed) with them
- Make a fake/real social media based on them

## Writing
- Make an AU (Actor/Coffee Shop/Roleswap/etc.)
- Make a wikipedia or website
- Fill out a [google doc template](https://www.tumblr.com/tagged/google%20docs%20template)
- Write small interactions
- Pick out tropes from [TV Tropes](https://tvtropes.org/) for them ([Flaws](https://tvtropes.org/pmwiki/pmwiki.php/Main/CharacterFlawIndex)/[Characterization](https://tvtropes.org/pmwiki/pmwiki.php/Main/CharacterizationTropes))
- Interview them ([My personal favorite template](https://www.rpnation.com/threads/hyperspecific-character-questions-for-your-entertainment.523119/))
- Make a 'box' for them filled with random trivia

## Drawing
- Make a [Shimeji](https://kilkakon.com/shimeji/)
- Make a [MMD](https://learnmmd.com/downloads/)/[Vroid](https://vroid.com/en/studio) Model
- Make a spinoff/slice of life comic series
- Make them a wallpaper/custom theme
- Make kandi based on their color scheme
- Make a felt plush of them
- Redraw them in a different piece of media (Anime/Cartoon/etc.)
- Dedicate a sketchbook to them
- Make a custom cursor
